About Me

I am a Field Researcher working at the intersection of ecology,Ecophysiology, biodiversity conservation, restoration science, and environmental monitoring. My work combines intensive field ecology with emerging spatial technologies to understand ecological processes across tropical landscapes.

Recently, I contribute to ecological research involving plant-pollinator interactions, long-term ecological monitoring, vegetation dynamics, and climate resilience of tropical forest ecosystems. I am also expanding my expertise in GIS and Remote Sensing for biodiversity assessment, landscape ecology, and carbon storage studies.
