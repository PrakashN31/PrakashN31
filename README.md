## About Me

I am a Field Researcher working at the intersection of ecology, Ecophysiology, biodiversity conservation, restoration science, and environmental monitoring. My work combines intensive field ecology with cutting-edge spatial analysis and data-driven approaches.

Recently, I contribute to ecological research involving plant-pollinator interactions, long-term ecological monitoring, vegetation dynamics, and climate resilience of tropical forest ecosystems.
